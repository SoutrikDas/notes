If we define $\phi$ and $\theta$ like so , then the condition for `just going to fall` is 

 

$$\phi = \theta$$

Which means we should find the tan , sin , cos of $\phi$ 

$$\tan \phi=\frac{\sqrt{R^2-r^2}}{r}$$

$$\sin \phi =\frac rR$$

$$\cos \phi=\frac{\sqrt{R^2-r^2}}{R}$$
