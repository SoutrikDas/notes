$$\begin{aligned}
\int \cot^5 x \quad dx&=\int \frac{\cos^5 x}{\sin^5x}  \quad dx\\
&=\int \frac{(1-t^2)^2}{t^4}  dt & t=\sin x \\
&=\int t^{-4}+1-2t^{-2} \ dt \\
&=-\frac{1}{3t^3}+t+2\frac1t+c \\
&=-\frac13 \cosec^3x +\sin x+2\cosec x +c

\end{aligned}$$
