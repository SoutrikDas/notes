$$\int \frac{x^2+1}{x^4-x^2+1} \ dx$$

$$\int \frac{1+\cfrac 1{x^2}}{x^2-2+\cfrac 1 {x^2} +1} \ dx$$

$$\int \frac{\overbrace{1+\cfrac 1{x^2}}^{\Large\text{dt}}}{\underbrace{\Big(x-\cfrac 1 {x}\Big)^2}_{\Large\text{ t}} +1} \ dx$$
